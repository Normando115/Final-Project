Artist Statement

This project is based on my experience working at a restaurant as a busser. It was made to emulate an buser at a restaurant and what you should and should not do. The goal of the game is to make sure you are making the right choices and not get fired. The reason I chose to make a game about my experience at a restaurant is because it was where this or that happens so I thought that I should make a game of that reality instead of actually something I genuinely enjoyed and sometimes when I worked there, I would imagine these scenarios doing some of these things that could get my boss angry. I hope you enjoy the game and the unique endings and scenarios that it holds.
